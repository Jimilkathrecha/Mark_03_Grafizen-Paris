How to get involved?

Each project is a unique project and therefore requires a very special and dedicated treatment. Along with the critical triangle of cost, time, and scope, the working model between client and agency also plays a crucial role in balancing the former. That's why, we have very robust, yet simple models - which are extremely flexible and customer-centric on the inside.

Budget constraint

If you have a well-defined project story and a full scope ready, we can give you a fixed cost and an expected schedule.

When to choose

Limited budget commitment

Very clear scope and stable set of requirements that do not are not likely to change throughout the project

Can set clear long-term milestones - whatever be the working models chosen (i.e. Agile or Waterfall)

Sometimes it works best when you're working also on a tight budget, to stay focused on features extremely critical that you cannot compromise with.

When you have less time for the team development, so you can concentrate on the commercial aspect of your business.

Evolving scope

Works best when you have a plan for a large project OR an ongoing project that requires continuous improvements - where iterations are eminent. We compose the best-in-class, multi-skilled team for you - working in closely with you, just like your own remote team.

When to choose

Scalable Scope Commitment

The scope is defined very briefly and the requirements are very likely to change throughout the project

Can set clear short-term milestones - depending on the chosen working model (i.e. Agile or Waterfall)

Works best when you're ready to perform multiple iterations for a given feature and refine it by inspecting all possible aspects.

When you have more time to devote to the team of development, sharing clear objectives and goals so that they are always in tune with you.

Seamless Workflow

Soft to the core. Organized to stay in sync.
